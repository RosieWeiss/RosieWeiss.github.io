Rosie Weiss, Violinist & Fiddler

1101 Stanford Drive

Summary

Originally from Billings, Montana, Rosie started playing fiddle at 3 years old, and won two state championships as a child. She was fortunate to learn from Natalie MacMaster, Bobby Hicks, Jeremy Kittel, and more. But she’s also a classical violinist, performing at Carnegie Hall, touring four continents, and now currently pursing a performance degree from the Frost School of Music.
